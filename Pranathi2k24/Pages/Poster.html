The Poster Project

Entry fee: ₹ 100


Unleash your imagination at the Collage Competition! Showcase your artistic skills by creating captivating collages from a variety of materials and images. Impress the audience and judges with your creativity, composition, and unique storytelling. Compete for top honors and let your collage art shine. Don't miss your chance to be a standout in the world of collage making!

Venue: AEC-3
No. of Participants: 1
Time Limit: 90 mins
No. of Teams per institution: 3
When: Day 1 (21-06-2024) 1:30PM to 2:30PM
Cash Prize: Winners-600 Runners-300

Rules

-ID Card is compulsory.
- Minimum 4 registrations compulsory for the event to be conducted
- Theme will be given on spot.
- Chart paper(A3 size) and glue will be provided.
- No other items will be provided. All the other required items like newspaper or magazine cutouts and scissors must be brought by the participants.
- Burning of papers is strictly prohibited.
- Participants will not be allowed to leave the hall during the event.
