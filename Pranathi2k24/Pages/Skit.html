The (Un) scripted tales(Skit)

Entry fee: ₹ 600


Step into the spotlight at The (Un)scripted Tales Skit Competition! Bring your creativity and acting skills to life with engaging and entertaining skits, whether scripted or improvised. Captivate the audience and judges with your humor, storytelling, and performance prowess. Compete for top honors and let your theatrical talent shine. Don't miss your chance to be a star in the world of skit performance!

Venue: Quadrangle
No. of Participants: 10 + 2 Narrators
No. of accompanists: 3 max
Time Limit: Max 10min Narrators(including stage clearance)
No. of Teams per institution: 2
When: Day 2 (22-06-2024) 11:30am-12:30pm
Cash Prize: Winners-2000 Runners-1000

Rules

- ID Card is compulsory.
- Minimum 4 registrations compulsory for the event to be conducted.
- Props and costumes are allowed.
- The performance should not be a part of any movie or TV show.
- Theme: Open theme
- Each team should submit one copy of synopsis(language English)at the reporting time.
- Vulgarity and obscenity in any form is not allowed.
