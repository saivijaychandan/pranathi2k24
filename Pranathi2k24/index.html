About Pranathi

Pranathi is the annual cultural fest of our college, celebrating creativity, talent, and community spirit. This vibrant event brings together students from diverse backgrounds to showcase their skills in music, dance, drama, and various arts. With a rich array of competitions, workshops, and performances, Pranathi fosters an environment of learning and camaraderie. Our mission is to inspire and empower students, providing a platform for self-expression and growth. Join us in this exciting journey as we celebrate the essence of youth and culture at Pranathi!

Once in Lifetime Experience

Pranathi was an unforgettable experience. The vibrant atmosphere, thrilling dance competition, and insightful workshops made it special. Each evening, captivating performances fostered a strong sense of community. Pranathi was a celebration of talent and creativity, leaving me with cherished memories and new friendships.
