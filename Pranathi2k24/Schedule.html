Duet Dance

Entry fee: ₹ 200


Join forces and light up the stage at the Duet Dance Competition! Showcase your chemistry and coordination through captivating routines across any dance style. Impress the audience and judges with your synchronized moves, creativity, and dynamic storytelling. Compete for top honors and let your partnership shine. Don't miss this chance to be the standout duo in the world of dance!

Venue: Quadrangle Stage
No. of Participants: 2
Time Limit: 4+2
No. of Teams per institution: 3
When: Day 1 (09-06-2023) 1pm-2pm
Cash Prize: Winners-1500 Runners-750

Rules

-ID Card is compulsory.
-Participating teams are responsible for removal of their sets,properties, belongings etc., immediately after the completion of their performance.
-No gender restriction(M+M or F+F or M+F)
-Judgment will be based on the basis of theme, rhythm, formation, expression, costumes, makeup, setting and on overall effect.
-Pre-recorded CDs or pen drives may be used.
